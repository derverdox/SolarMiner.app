# Finance & ROI Amortization

Is your solar mining operation actually profitable? The **Finance Dashboard** gives you the exact answer by factoring in hardware costs, Bitcoin earnings, and crucial opportunity costs[cite: 19].

## 💰 Investment Tracking
To calculate an accurate Return on Investment (ROI), you can log your initial setup costs:
* **PV System Cost:** Enter the total cost and setup date of your solar array.
* **Hardware Costs:** Assign individual purchase prices to each ASIC miner in your cluster.

The dashboard combines these into a **Total Investment** KPI.

## 📅 Daily Amortization & Net Profit
The core of the Finance page is the historical amortization grid. For every day of operation, the app calculates your **Net Mining Profit** by processing:
1. **Power Usage:** Total kWh consumed by your miners.
2. **BTC Earnings:** The actual Bitcoin rewarded by your pool.
3. **Gross Revenue:** The fiat value of your mined Bitcoin on that specific day.
4. **Missed Export Earnings:** The amount of fiat money you *would have earned* if you sold that consumed power to the grid.

**Net Profit** = Gross Revenue - Missed Export Earnings.

## 📊 Global ROI Progress
By taking your absolute net profit and comparing it against your total hardware investments, the dashboard displays a real-time **ROI Progress percentage**, showing you exactly how close you are to breaking even.

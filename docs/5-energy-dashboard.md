# Live Energy & PV Dashboard

The **Energy Dashboard** is the command center for your solar mining operation[cite: 17]. It provides a comprehensive, real-time overview of your entire local energy ecosystem, ensuring you always know exactly where your electricity is flowing[cite: 21, 22].

## ⚡ Real-Time Energy Flow
At the top of the dashboard, four critical metrics update live as your solar inverter reports data[cite: 17, 21]:
* **PV Generation:** The total solar power currently being produced by your panels in kilowatts (kW)[cite: 17].
* **House Consumption:** The base load of your home, completely separate from your mining hardware[cite: 17, 22].
* **Grid Export:** The excess power being sent to the public grid[cite: 17, 22].
* **Grid Import:** The power being pulled from the grid when solar generation is insufficient[cite: 17, 22].

## 🔋 Battery Status & Power Charts
If you have a home battery integrated, the dashboard features a dedicated **Battery Card**[cite: 22]. This displays the live State of Charge (SoC) in percent, alongside the current charge or discharge power in kW[cite: 22]. 

Additionally, historical charts map out your PV power, import, export, and dedicated miner consumption over the course of the day[cite: 21].

## 💡 Opportunity Cost Breakdown
The Solar Miner App goes beyond simple watts and kilowatts by calculating the financial reality of your energy usage[cite: 22]. The dashboard calculates:
* **Avoided Import Costs:** The fiat money you saved by powering your home with solar instead of buying from the grid[cite: 22].
* **Lost Export Revenue:** The exact amount of feed-in tariff (e.g., in EUR or USD) you sacrificed by routing your solar surplus to your ASICs instead of the grid[cite: 22].

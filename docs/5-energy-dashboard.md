# Live Energy & PV Dashboard

The **Energy Dashboard** is the command center for your solar mining operation. It provides a comprehensive, real-time overview of your entire local energy ecosystem, ensuring you always know exactly where your electricity is flowing.

## ⚡ Real-Time Energy Flow
At the top of the dashboard, four critical metrics update live as your solar inverter reports data:
* **PV Generation:** The total solar power currently being produced by your panels in kilowatts (kW).
* **House Consumption:** The base load of your home, completely separate from your mining hardware.
* **Grid Export:** The excess power being sent to the public grid.
* **Grid Import:** The power being pulled from the grid when solar generation is insufficient.

## 🔋 Battery Status & Power Charts
If you have a home battery integrated, the dashboard features a dedicated **Battery Card**. This displays the live State of Charge (SoC) in percent, alongside the current charge or discharge power in kW. 

Additionally, historical charts map out your PV power, import, export, and dedicated miner consumption over the course of the day.

## 💡 Opportunity Cost Breakdown
The Solar Miner App goes beyond simple watts and kilowatts by calculating the financial reality of your energy usage. The dashboard calculates:
* **Avoided Import Costs:** The fiat money you saved by powering your home with solar instead of buying from the grid.
* **Lost Export Revenue:** The exact amount of feed-in tariff (e.g., in EUR or USD) you sacrificed by routing your solar surplus to your ASICs instead of the grid.

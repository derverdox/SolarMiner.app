# ASIC Telemetry & Pool Statistics

The **Mining Dashboard** is your dedicated control interface for all connected Bitcoin mining hardware and mining pools[cite: 20].

## 🖥️ Live Hardware Telemetry
Monitor the health and performance of every miner in your cluster simultaneously[cite: 20]. For each ASIC, the app displays:
* **Live Hashrate:** Track your performance in TH/s[cite: 20].
* **Power Consumption:** View the exact live power draw in Watts[cite: 20].
* **Temperature:** Monitor hardware safety with live Celsius readings[cite: 20].
* **Status Controls:** Manually override your automation rules to instantly pause or resume mining[cite: 20].

## 📈 Global Efficiency Metrics
The dashboard aggregates your cluster's performance into overarching KPIs[cite: 20]:
* Total Hashrate (TH/s)[cite: 20].
* Total Power Draw (W)[cite: 20].
* **Mining Efficiency:** Calculated in real-time as Watts per Terahash (W/TH), allowing you to quickly spot hardware degradation or optimize your firmware tuning[cite: 20].

## 🔗 Mining Pool Integration
Connect your Braiins Pool account to view your payout history directly alongside your hardware data[cite: 20]. The dashboard highlights active pool connections, your latest Bitcoin earnings, and your total 90-day accumulated payouts[cite: 20].
